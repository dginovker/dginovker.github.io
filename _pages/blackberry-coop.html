About BlackBerry

BlackBerry Limited, previously known as Research In Motion, is a Canadian tech company that focuses on secure enterprise software and the Internet of things. Founded in 1984 by Mike Lazaridis and Douglas Fregin, BlackBerry has become a multinational corporation best known for their BlackBerry devices and their cybersecurity.
